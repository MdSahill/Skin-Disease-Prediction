About SkinCheck

Technology Behind the Project

SkinCheck uses a deep residual neural network (ResNet) trained on thousands of dermatological images to classify skin conditions with high accuracy.

Model Specifications

  • Architecture: Custom ResNet with dropout layers
  • Input Size: 224x224 RGB images
  • Classes: 22 different skin conditions
  • Training: Adam optimizer with cross-entropy loss
